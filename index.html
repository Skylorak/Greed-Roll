How to Play Greed Roll

You can play by yourself or with friends.

  1. This game is designed for 2 players. Each player’s goal is to be the first to reach 100 points.
  2. You may ask: “How do you win?” — The answer: score 100 points before your opponent.
  3. Your next question might be: “Where can I see my points?” Below Player 1 and Player 2, you start with a value of 0. That’s your score counter.
  4. Under your score, you’ll see Current with a value of 0. This shows the points you’ve earned in the current turn, which you can choose to roll again or bank.
  5. In the middle of the screen, you’ll find three buttons: New Game, Roll Dice, and Bank.
  6. Press New Game to restart the game from the beginning.
  7. Press Roll Dice to roll the die and earn points from 2 to 6. As you keep rolling, your current score increases. Use this strategy to reach 100 points.
  8. If you don’t want to risk losing your current points, press Bank. Your current points will be added to your total score under Player 1 or Player 2 (depending on whose turn it is). After banking, the game automatically switches to the next player.
  9. ⚠️Important! If you roll a 1, you lose all the points from your current turn, and it immediately becomes your opponent’s turn.
  10. If you win, your player’s name will turn gold and your side will glow.
